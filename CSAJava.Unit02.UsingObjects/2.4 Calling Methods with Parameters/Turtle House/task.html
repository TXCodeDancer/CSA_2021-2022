Programming Challenge : Turtle House repl.it

Follow the repl.it link, fork the solution and try the following tasks.

This creative challenge is fun to do collaboratively in pairs. Design a house and have the turtle draw it with different colors (use this repl.it link). Can you add windows and a door? Come up with your own house design as a team.

To draw a window, you will need to do penUp() to walk the turtle into position, for example:
      builder.penUp();
      builder.moveTo(120,200);
      builder.penDown();
